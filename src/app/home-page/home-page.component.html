What is this?

TraceMap gives you the possibillity to follow the flow of a Tweet through Twitter. It will show you who retweeted the post from which user and gives you an insight of how the users are connected with each other.

By now TraceMap is restricted by Twitter to the last 100 retweets of a tweet. Though Tweets with more than 100 retweets will be displayed incomplete. Some of the distributors might not be shown and some connections will be missing.

How it works

1. Find a Tweet on Twitter, you like to trace. Click on the Dropdown-Button of the Tweet.

2. Press „Copy Url“ and paste it in the Search-Field of TraceMap.
