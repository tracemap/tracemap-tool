TRACEMAP

Our approach

In three major milestones we want to give Twitter users information about the trustworthiness of news.

1. We provide a tool for the visualisation of news diffusion inside Twitter. People can track the propagation of each tweet and analyze the most relevant metrics.

2. Interactive features equip users to better investigate what they read online, ultimately enabling them to share their findings with others. With the ability to add tags and links to external resources TraceMap becomes a crowd-debunking platform.

3. Based on the scientific literature, we use well-known examples of news to train our machine-learning algorithms. The news-diffusion patterns allow us to estimate the trustworthiness of every piece of information on Twitter.

Code of conduct

We believe the crowd-debunking effectiveness of TraceMap can only be achieved if the tool is free of charge. In addition, we are completely open source, our code is available on github.
