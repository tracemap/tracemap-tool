Open Philosophy

We are interested in the development of methodologies and models for collaboration and resource sharing, to which receiving support and merging like-minded networks add immense value. As an initiative, we are ready to share our experience in the application of technologies to tackle social challenges. If you are interested in stimulating a culture of open knowledge, open source and open data, please contact us!

We believe the effectiveness of TraceMap can only be achieved if the tool is free of charge. Not only is our aim to make news diffusion transparent, we are also transparent in our work. Our codes and algorithms are open source.
